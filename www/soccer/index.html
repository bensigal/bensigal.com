Instructions

  • Paint as many squares as you can in 30 seconds.
  • Paint a square by moving over it.
  • Player one moves with WASD.
  • Player two moves with the arrow keys.
  • Whoever has more squares at the end of 30 seconds wins.
  • However, if you tag your opponent while they're intruding on your side, you win.
  • Press space to begin, and to restart.
